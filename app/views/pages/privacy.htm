The mission of Vivarena is to connect the business world’s entrepreneurs, investors, advisors, professionals, and service providers to meet, communicate, learn, collaborate, find opportunities and make better decisions leveraging their network of trusted social relationships.

Registering with Vivarena means sharing information about yourself with others, communicating with them, and working privately. Your account is initially set up to share the information that we believe the majority of our Users are interested in sharing. But the type and amount of information you decide to share, and with whom you share it, is up to you.

Information about you consists of:

Profile Information. This is the information that your connections, other members, and companies see about you. This includes your profile information and may also include any files, images, links or documents you may have uploaded. You can update your information at any time and you can decide whom you share your information with.

Communications, Messaging and Sharing. Vivarena is designed to help you communicate with your connections. Some of this is one-to-one, some of it is in groups, and some of it is public discussion. We receive information about you when you communicate with others as well as when others communicate with you or about you. This includes your contributions to other community features Vivarena may offer such as “Suggestions” and “Polls.” You decide how much you wish to communicate and in what forum.

Activity and Behavior. Your information also includes the information you create by your activity on the site, such as when you post a status update, add a connection to your network, make a suggestion or answer a poll. In addition to the content included in your behavior, your information may include the behavioral act itself. We also provide tools that you can use privately on Vivarena (for example, searching for another member). You decide who sees your activity on the site.

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by means of a notice on our home page so that you may access and review the changes to this document in advance of your continued use of the service. If you object to any changes, you may close your account. By continuing to use the Vivarena service after notice of changes has been sent to you or published on the Vivarena website, you are consenting to the changes.

      Like most websites, we use cookies and web log files to track site usage and trends, to improve the quality of our service, to customize your experience on Vivarena. A cookie is a tiny data file that resides on your computer, mobile phone, or other device, and allows us to recognize you as a User when you return to the Vivarena website using the same computer and web browser. You can remove or block cookies using the settings in your browser, but in some cases doing so may impact your ability to use Vivarena. A cookie is set once you’ve logged in to your Vivarena account. The next time you visit the Vivarena website, the cookie will allow us to recognize you as an existing User so you will not need to log in before using the Services. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
